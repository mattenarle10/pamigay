Our Advocacy

Fighting hunger and food waste through technology and community action

Our Mission

At Pamigay, we believe that no good food should go to waste when it could nourish someone in need. Our mission is to create a more equitable food system by connecting excess food with those who need it most.

Through our platform, we empower restaurants and food businesses to donate their surplus food instead of throwing it away, while enabling community organizations to efficiently access and distribute these donations.

Reducing Food Waste

Millions of tons of edible food are wasted annually while people go hungry. We're changing that by creating an efficient donation system.

Feeding Communities

We help connect surplus food with community organizations that serve vulnerable populations and those experiencing food insecurity.

Environmental Impact

By reducing food waste, we help decrease greenhouse gas emissions and conserve the resources used in food production.

Supporting Businesses

We help food businesses reduce waste disposal costs while making a positive social impact in their communities.

How It Works

A simple process that makes a big difference

1

Donation

Restaurants list their surplus food through our mobile app, specifying details like quantity, type, and pickup window.

2

Discovery

Verified community organizations browse available donations in their area using search and filter options.

3

Pickup

Organizations request pickups, receive confirmation, and collect the food donations at the scheduled time.

4

Distribution

Organizations distribute the food to those in need, creating a more sustainable and equitable food system.

Mobile App

Connecting donors and recipients with just a few taps

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for both donors and recipients
  • Real-time notifications for donation requests and updates
  • Location tracking for efficient pickup coordination
  • Search and filter functionality to find relevant donations
  • Verification system to ensure legitimacy and build trust
  • Donation history and activity tracking
